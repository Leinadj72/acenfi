About Us

ACENFI is a forward-thinking, Africa-based organization dedicated to advancing the adoption and integration of fintech innovations, inclusive financial services, sustainable digital payment systems, and Digital MSME across the continent. Our team consists of seasoned experts, leaders, and advisors with deep regional knowledge, collaborating closely with institutions, governments, and private sector stakeholders to foster financial inclusion, drive digital transformation, and empower African economies. Through our tailored solutions and strategic partnerships, ACENFI aims to bridge gaps in the financial ecosystem, ensuring all Africans have access to secure, efficient, and innovative financial services.

Our Values

ACENFI is an African-led organization committed to driving the development of innovative, inclusive, and sustainable financial solutions across Africa. Our work focuses on transforming the digital payments landscape, enabling the growth of fintech, digital finance, and payment systems that provide equitable access to all, including marginalized communities. Leveraging the expertise of our diverse team and our extensive network of regional partners, we support institutions, governments, and the private sector in creating systems that foster financial inclusion, boost economic growth, and empower local communities.

At ACENFI, our actions are guided by seven core values, thus, Collaboration, Innovation, Integrity, Inclusivity, Sustainability, Excellence, and Knowledge. These values are the foundation of our efforts to drive meaningful change across Africa‚Äôs digital financial ecosystem, ensuring that our work aligns with our vision of a digitally empowered, financially inclusive continent.

At ACENFI, our actions are guided by these core principles:

  • Collaboration: We foster collaboration through partnerships, believing that collective efforts lead to impactful and sustainable transformations in Africa's financial ecosystem.
  • Innovation: We drive innovation by developing forward-thinking, technology-driven solutions that address emerging challenges in digital finance and payment systems.
  • Integrity: We maintain integrity in all interactions, ensuring transparency, honesty, and ethical decision-making in every facet of our work.
  • Inclusivity: We champion inclusivity by striving to provide equitable access to digital financial services for all, particularly the underserved and excluded communities.
  • Sustainability: We focus on creating sustainable solutions that promote long-term economic growth, environmental responsibility, and social well-being for future generations.
  • Excellence: We pursue excellence by delivering exceptional services, maintaining the highest standards of quality and ensuring results that exceed expectations.
  • Knowledge: We empower decision-makers by fostering a culture of continuous learning, sharing valuable insights, and building expertise across the fintech and payments sectors.
