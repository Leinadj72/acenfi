Where We Work

We advise, research, and partner with diverse stakeholders to drive impactful fintech and digital finance innovations, accelerate economic growth, and enhance payment systems across Africa. Our mission is to provide expert guidance, strategic insights, and actionable solutions that ensure the long-term success and transformation of the digital finance landscape.

Government Bodies and Regulators

We collaborate with government agencies, central banks, and regulators to shape policies that promote inclusive digital finance and payment systems, ensuring the accessibility, security, and alignment of financial systems with national development objectives.

Regional and Economic Stakeholders

We assist regional economic communities in optimizing cross-border digital financial frameworks, fostering integration, and developing instant and inclusive payment solutions to drive economic cooperation and growth.

Private Sector and Fintech Enterprises

We work alongside private sector players, fintech, and digital finance companies to craft strong business models for inclusive payment systems, ensuring alignment with market demands, compliance with regulations, and fostering technological innovation.

Development Partners and Non-Governmental Organizations

We partner with development organizations and NGOs to tackle fintech, digital finance, and financial inclusion challenges through research, advocacy, and capacity building. We strive to establish long-term, inclusive financial ecosystems that benefit underserved communities.

Academic Institutions and Research Organizations

We collaborate with universities and research centers to encourage innovation and knowledge-sharing, empowering the next generation of fintech professionals and promoting research-driven approaches to digital finance solutions.
