What We Do

Africa's financial ecosystem faces challenges in scaling and sustaining fintech innovations, digital finance, and inclusive payment systems limiting broader economic growth and financial inclusion.

At Acenfi, we specialize in enhancing Africa‚Äôs financial ecosystem by supporting the development, scaling, and sustainability of fintech innovations, digital financial services, and inclusive payment systems.

We provide strategic support from the early stages to post-implementation, focusing on goal-setting, work plan development, economic feasibility, and ongoing monitoring to ensure continued success, sustainability, and effective scaling of fintech and payment system initiatives

Our Services

Project Planning and Structuring

  • Project Goal Definition: Helping project owners define clear objectives and success metrics to ensure that fintech, digital finance and payment system initiatives are aligned with broader business and development goals.
  • Work Plan Structuring: Guiding the design of comprehensive work plans, outlining milestones, timelines, and resource requirements to optimize project implementation.
  • Economic Feasibility Analysis: Assessing the financial viability of proposed projects, using robust financial modelling tools to determine the long-term sustainability and potential return on investment.

We enhance the capacity of African institutions by providing technical expertise, building institutional capabilities, and offering specialized training programs to support professionals and the successful implementation and scaling of fintech, digital finance, and payment system solutions across the continent.

Capacity Enhancement

  • Technical Expertise Provision: Provision of skilled technical experts to assist African institutions in implementing and scaling fintech, digital finance and payment solutions building local capacity and reducing dependence on external support.
  • Institutional Capacity Building: Working with pan-African institutions to enhance their ability to manage and support fintech, digital finance and fintech projects through tailored training programs and knowledge-sharing platforms.
  • Targeted Training Programs: Offering of specialized training to develop the next generation of fintech, digital finance and payment system professionals, helping individuals and organizations build the skills required for successful digital transformation.

We provide actionable research, policy recommendations, strategic guidance and consultancy to help organizations and governments develop sustainable fintech solutions, inclusive financial systems, and secure digital payment infrastructure across Africa.

Research and Consultancy

  • Actionable Research: Conducting in-depth research and consultancy on fintech trends, digital finance, digital MSMEs, and financial inclusion, providing valuable insights and recommendations to inform policy and business strategies.
  • Policy Recommendations: Research-driven consultancy to help stakeholders develop effective policies that promote inclusive finance, secure digital payment systems, and sustainable fintech ecosystems across Africa.
  • Strategy Development: Assisting organizations and governments in developing long-term strategies to scale digital financial services and payment systems, ensuring they meet local needs and regulatory requirements.

We advocate for regulatory reforms, engage key stakeholders, and promote collaboration to foster the growth of inclusive fintech, digital finance, and secure payment systems across Africa.

Advocacy & Engagement

  • Regulatory Advocacy: Leading advocate for regulatory reforms and consumer protection initiatives that support the growth of inclusive and secure fintech, digital finance and payment solutions, working with policymakers and regulatory bodies to create favourable environments for innovation.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Convening key industry players, including financial institutions, technology providers, and government agencies, to foster collaboration and drive the adoption of fintech, digital finance solutions and payments
  • Collaboration Promotion: Facilitating partnerships and public-private sector collaborations that aim to create scalable, fintech, digital finance, and payments solutions that benefit diverse populations across Africa.
